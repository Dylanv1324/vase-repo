LESSON 1: The Planets
----------------------

Our Solar System
----------------
The solar system consists of the Sun and everything that orbits around it.
This includes 8 planets, their moons, asteroids, and comets.

The Eight Planets (in order from the Sun):
-------------------------------------------
1. Mercury - Smallest and closest to the Sun
2. Venus - Hottest planet
3. Earth - Our home planet
4. Mars - The Red Planet
5. Jupiter - Largest planet
6. Saturn - Has beautiful rings
7. Uranus - Rotates on its side
8. Neptune - Farthest from the Sun

Practice:
---------
1. Which planet is closest to the Sun?
2. Which is the largest planet?
3. What makes Saturn special?

Answers: 1) Mercury  2) Jupiter  3) It has beautiful rings
